Hotdog

Description

nice flavors with very tender meat. this is the way i normally make hot sausage except for the slow cooker (why didn't i think of that before?). i did boil my sausage for about 20 minutes ahead of time to remove some of the fat.

I used this recipe as an idea ONLY. I cannot imagine the grease factor with sausages in a crock pot for hours on end. I used bulk sweet Italian sausage, crumbled into smallish almost walnut sized chunks and browned in a bit of olive oil.

Ingredients

  • 8 (4 ounce) links fresh Italian sausage
  • 1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place Italian sausage links, spaghetti sauce, green pepper, and onion into a slow cooker; mix until well combined.
  3. Cover and cook on Low for 6 hours. Serve on hoagie rolls.
